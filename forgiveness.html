رَبِّ اغْفِرْ لِي وَتُبْ عَلَيَّ، إِنَّكَ أَنْتَ التَّوَّابُ الرَّحِيمُ

Rabbighfir lī wa tub ʿalayya, innaka anta at-Tawwābu ar-Raḥīm.

My Lord, forgive me and accept my repentance. Indeed, You are the Accepting of Repentance, the Merciful.

[Tirmidhi]

اللَّهُمَّ إِنَّكَ عَفُوٌّ تُحِبُّ الْعَفْوَ فَاعْفُ عَنِّي

Allāhumma innaka ʿafuwwun tuḥibbu al-ʿafwa faʿfu ʿannī.

O Allah, You are Pardoning and You love pardon, so pardon me.

[Tirmidhi]

رَبَّنَا ظَلَمْنَا أَنفُسَنَا وَإِن لَّمْ تَغْفِرْ لَنَا وَتَرْحَمْنَا لَنَكُونَنَّ مِنَ الْخَاسِرِينَ

Rabbana ẓalamnā anfusanā wa illam taghfir lanā wa tarḥamnā lanakūnanna mina al-khāsirīn.

Our Lord, we have wronged ourselves. If You do not forgive us and have mercy upon us, we will surely be among the losers.

[Surah Al-A’raf 7:23]

اللَّهُمَّ اغْفِرْ لِي ذَنْبِي كُلَّهُ، دِقَّهُ وَجِلَّهُ، وَأَوَّلَهُ وَآخِرَهُ، وَعَلَانِيَتَهُ وَسِرَّهُ

Allāhumma ighfir lī dhanbī kullahu, diqqahu wa jillahu, wa awwalahu wa ākhirahu, wa ʿalāniyatahu wa sirrahu.

O Allah, forgive all my sins — the minor and major, the first and last, the open and secret.

[Muslim]

رَبِّ اغْفِرْ لِي، رَبِّ اغْفِرْ لِي

Rabbighfir lī, Rabbighfir lī.

My Lord, forgive me. My Lord, forgive me.

[Bukhari & Muslim]

أَسْتَغْفِرُ اللَّهَ الَّذِي لَا إِلٰهَ إِلَّا هُوَ الْحَيُّ الْقَيُّومُ وَأَتُوبُ إِلَيْهِ

Astaghfirullāha alladhī lā ilāha illā huwa al-ḥayyul-qayyūm wa atūbu ilayh.

I seek forgiveness from Allah, there is no deity but Him, the Ever-Living, the Sustainer, and I repent to Him.

[Tirmidhi]

رَبِّ هَبْ لِي حُكْمًا وَأَلْحِقْنِي بِالصَّالِحِينَ

Rabbi hab lī ḥukman wa alḥiqnī biṣ-ṣāliḥīn.

My Lord, grant me wisdom and join me with the righteous.

[Surah Ash-Shu‘ara 26:83]

اللَّهُمَّ اغْفِرْ لِي وَارْحَمْنِي وَاهْدِنِي وَعَافِنِي وَارْزُقْنِي

Allāhumma ighfir lī warḥamnī wahdinī wa ʿāfinī warzuqnī.

O Allah, forgive me, have mercy on me, guide me, grant me well-being, and provide for me.

[Muslim]

اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي ظَلَمْتُ نَفْسِي ظُلْمًا كَثِيرًا

Allāhumma innī ẓalamtu nafsī ẓulman kathīran.

O Allah, I have greatly wronged myself.

[Bukhari & Muslim]

اللَّهُمَّ اغْفِرْ لِي خَطِيئَتِي وَجَهْلِي وَإِسْرَافِي فِي أَمْرِي

Allāhumma ighfir lī khaṭī’atī wa jahli wa isrāfī fī amrī.

O Allah, forgive me my mistakes, ignorance, and excesses in all my affairs.

[Bukhari & Muslim]
