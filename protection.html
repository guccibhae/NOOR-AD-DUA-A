بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الَّذِي لَا يَضُرُّ مَعَ اسْمِهِ شَيْءٌ فِي الْأَرْضِ وَلَا فِي السَّمَاءِ وَهُوَ السَّمِيعُ الْعَلِيمُ

Bismillāhil-ladhī lā yaḍurru ma‘a ismihi shay’un fī al-arḍi wa lā fī as-samā’ wa huwa as-Samī‘ul-‘Alīm.

In the name of Allah with whose name nothing on earth or in the heavens can cause harm, and He is the All-Hearing, All-Knowing.

— Abu Dawood 5088, At-Tirmidhi 3388

اللَّهُمَّ احْفَظْنِي مِنْ بَيْنِ يَدَيَّ وَمِنْ خَلْفِي، وَعَنْ يَمِينِي، وَعَنْ شِمَالِي، وَمِنْ فَوْقِي، وَأَعُوذُ بِعَظَمَتِكَ أَنْ أُغْتَالَ مِنْ تَحْتِي

Allāhumma iḥfaẓnī min bayni yadayya wa min khalfī, wa ‘an yamīnī wa ‘an shimālī, wa min fawqī, wa a‘ūdhu bi-‘aẓamatika an ughtāla min taḥtī.

O Allah, protect me from in front of me, from behind me, on my right, on my left, and from above me. I seek refuge in Your greatness from being struck from beneath me.

— Ahmad 2/92, Abu Dawood 5074

اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنَ الْبَرَصِ، وَالْجُنُونِ، وَالْجُذَامِ، وَمِنْ سَيِّئِ الْأَسْقَامِ

Allāhumma innī a‘ūdhu bika minal-baras, wal-junūn, wal-judhām, wa min sayyi’il-asqām.

O Allah, I seek refuge with You from vitiligo, madness, leprosy, and evil diseases.

— Abu Dawood 1554

أَعُوذُ بِكَلِمَاتِ اللَّهِ التَّامَّاتِ مِنْ شَرِّ مَا خَلَقَ

A‘ūdhu bikalimātillāhit-tāmmāti min sharri mā khalaq.

I seek refuge in the Perfect Words of Allah from the evil of what He has created.

— Muslim 2708

اللَّهُمَّ اكْفِنِيهِمْ بِمَا شِئْتَ

Allāhumma ikfinīhim bimā shi’ta.

O Allah, protect me from them however You wish.

— Bukhari 4563

حَسْبُنَا اللَّهُ وَنِعْمَ الْوَكِيلُ

Ḥasbunallāhu wa ni‘ma al-wakīl.

Allah is Sufficient for us, and He is the Best Disposer of affairs.

— Quran 3:173

اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنَ الْهَمِّ وَالْحَزَنِ، وَالْعَجْزِ وَالْكَسَلِ، وَالْجُبْنِ وَالْبُخْلِ، وَغَلَبَةِ الدَّيْنِ، وَقَهْرِ الرِّجَالِ

Allāhumma innī a‘ūdhu bika minal-hammi wal-ḥazan, wal-‘ajzi wal-kasal, wal-jubni wal-bukhl, wa ghalabatid-dayni wa qahrir-rijāl.

O Allah, I seek refuge in You from worry and grief, weakness and laziness, cowardice and stinginess, being overwhelmed by debt, and overpowered by men.

— Bukhari 2893

رَبِّ أَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنْ هَمَزَاتِ الشَّيَاطِينِ، وَأَعُوذُ بِكَ رَبِّ أَنْ يَحْضُرُونِ

Rabbi a‘ūdhu bika min hamazātish-shayāṭīn, wa a‘ūdhu bika rabbi an yaḥḍurūn.

My Lord! I seek refuge in You from the whisperings of the devils. And I seek refuge in You, my Lord, lest they be present with me.

— Quran 23:97-98

اللَّهُمَّ احْفَظْنِي وَأَهْلِي مِنْ كُلِّ سُوءٍ وَمَكْرِ الْمَاكِرِينَ

Allāhumma iḥfaẓnī wa ahlī min kulli sū’ wa makri al-mākirīn.

O Allah, protect me and my family from every evil and the plots of the deceivers.

— General Du’a (from classical supplications)

اللَّهُمَّ أَنْتَ وَلِيِّي فِي الدُّنْيَا وَالآخِرَةِ، تَوَفَّنِي مُسْلِمًا وَأَلْحِقْنِي بِالصَّالِحِينَ

Allāhumma anta walīyyī fī ad-dunyā wal-ākhirah, tawaffanī musliman wa alḥiqnī biṣ-ṣāliḥīn.

O Allah, You are my Guardian in this world and the Hereafter. Take my soul as a Muslim and join me with the righteous.

— Quran 12:101
