اللَّهُمَّ اهْدِنِي وَسَدِّدْنِي

Allahumma ihdinee wa saddidnee

O Allah, guide me and make me steadfast.

[Muslim 2725]

رَبِّ هَبْ لِي حُكْمًا وَأَلْحِقْنِي بِالصَّالِحِينَ

Rabbi hab li ḥukman wa alḥiqni biṣ-ṣāliḥīn

My Lord, grant me wisdom and join me with the righteous.

[Surah Ash-Shu'ara 26:83]

رَبِّ اشْرَحْ لِي صَدْرِي * وَيَسِّرْ لِي أَمْرِي

Rabbi ishraḥ lī ṣadrī * wa yassir lī amrī

My Lord, expand for me my chest and ease my task for me.

[Surah Taha 20:25-26]

رَبَّنَا لَا تُزِغْ قُلُوبَنَا بَعْدَ إِذْ هَدَيْتَنَا

Rabbana la tuzigh quloobana ba‘da idh hadaytana

Our Lord, do not let our hearts deviate after You have guided us.

[Surah Al Imran 3:8]

اللَّهُمَّ اجْعَلْنِي مِنْ مَنْ تَهْدِيهِمْ وَتُرْشِدُهُمْ

Allahumma aj‘alni min man tahdīhim wa turshiduhum

O Allah, make me among those whom You guide and direct.

[General Du’a – Authentic Phrase]

اللَّهُمَّ اهْدِنِي لِأَحْسَنِ الْأَخْلَاقِ، لَا يَهْدِي لِأَحْسَنِهَا إِلَّا أَنْتَ

Allahumma ihdinee li aḥsanil akhlāq, la yahdi li aḥsaniha illa Anta

O Allah, guide me to the best of manners; none guides to the best of them except You.

[Muslim 771]

رَبِّ زِدْنِي عِلْمًا

Rabbi zidni ‘ilma

My Lord, increase me in knowledge.

[Surah Taha 20:114]

اللَّهُمَّ أَرِنَا الْحَقَّ حَقًّا وَارْزُقْنَا اتِّبَاعَهُ

Allahumma arina al-ḥaqqa ḥaqqan warzuqna ittibā‘ah

O Allah, show us the truth as truth and bless us to follow it.

[General Du’a – Authentic Concept]

رَبِّ اجْعَلْنِي مُقِيمَ الصَّلَاةِ وَمِنْ ذُرِّيَّتِي

Rabbi aj‘alni muqīmaṣ-ṣalāh wa min dhurriyyati

My Lord, make me an establisher of prayer, and [many] from my descendants.

[Surah Ibrahim 14:40]

وَمَن يُؤْمِن بِاللَّهِ يَهْدِ قَلْبَهُ

Wa man yu’min billāhi yahdi qalbah

And whoever believes in Allah – He will guide his heart.

[Surah At-Taghabun 64:11]
