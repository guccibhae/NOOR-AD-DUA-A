اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنَ الْهَمِّ وَالْحَزَنِ...
Allahumma inni a‘udhu bika minal-hammi wal-ḥazani...
O Allah, I seek refuge in You from anxiety and sorrow...
[Bukhari 6369]
حَسْبُنَا اللَّهُ وَنِعْمَ الْوَكِيلُ
Hasbunallahu wa ni‘mal-wakeel
Allah is Sufficient for us...
[Qur'an 3:173]
إِنَّ مَعَ الْعُسْرِ يُسْرًا
Inna ma‘al-‘usri yusra
Indeed, with hardship comes ease.
[Qur’an 94:6]
وَعَسَىٰ أَن تَكْرَهُوا شَيْئًا وَهُوَ خَيْرٌ لَّكُمْ
Wa ‘asa an takrahu shay’an wahuwa khayrun lakum
Perhaps you dislike a thing and it is good for you.
[Qur’an 2:216]
اللَّهُمَّ رَحْمَتَكَ أَرْجُو فَلَا تَكِلْنِي...
Allahumma rahmataka arju fa la takilni ila nafsi tarfata ‘ayn...
O Allah, I hope for Your mercy, do not leave me to myself even for the blink of an eye...
[Abu Dawud 5090]
اللَّهُمَّ اجْبُرْ كَسْرِي وَارْحَمْ ضَعْفِي
Allahumma ajbur kasri warham da‘fi
O Allah, mend my brokenness and have mercy on my weakness.
[Attributed to classical scholars]
لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا أَنْتَ سُبْحَانَكَ، إِنِّي كُنْتُ مِنَ الظَّالِمِينَ
La ilaha illa anta subhanaka inni kuntu minaẓ-ẓalimeen
There is no deity except You; exalted are You. Indeed, I have been of the wrongdoers.
[Qur’an 21:87]
رَبِّ اشْرَحْ لِي صَدْرِي
Rabbi ishrah li sadri
My Lord, expand for me my chest.
[Qur’an 20:25]
اللَّهُمَّ لَا سَهْلَ إِلَّا مَا جَعَلْتَهُ سَهْلًا...
Allahumma la sahla illa ma ja‘altahu sahlan...
O Allah, nothing is easy except what You make easy...
[Ibn Hibban 2427]
اللَّهُمَّ اجْعَلْ القُرْآنَ رَبِيعَ قَلْبِي
Allahumma aj‘al al-Qur’an rabi‘a qalbi
O Allah, make the Qur’an the spring of my heart...
[Ahmad 3712]
