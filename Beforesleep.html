بِاسْمِكَ اللَّهُمَّ أَمُوتُ وَأَحْيَا

Bismika Allahumma amootu wa ahyaa

In Your name, O Allah, I die and I live.

(Bukhari)

اللَّهُمَّ قِنِي عَذَابَكَ يَوْمَ تَبْعَثُ عِبَادَكَ

Allahumma qinee ‘adhaabaka yawma tab’athu ‘ibaadak

O Allah, protect me from Your punishment on the Day You resurrect Your servants.

(Tirmidhi)

اللَّهُمَّ بِاسْمِكَ أَمُوتُ وَأَحْيَا

Allahumma bismika amootu wa ahyaa

O Allah, in Your name I die and live.

(Bukhari)

اللَّهُمَّ أَسْلَمْتُ نَفْسِي إِلَيْكَ

Allahumma aslamtu nafsee ilayk

O Allah, I submit myself to You.

(Bukhari & Muslim)

اللَّهُمَّ اغْفِرْ لِي ذَنْبِي وَأَخْسِئْ شَيْطَانِي

Allahumma ighfir lee dhanbee wa akhsi’ shaytaanee

O Allah, forgive me my sins and drive away my devil.

(Tirmidhi)

الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ الَّذِي أَحْيَانَا بَعْدَ مَا أَمَاتَنَا

Alhamdu lillaahil-lathee ahyaanaa ba’da maa amaatanaa

All praise is for Allah who gave us life after having taken it from us.

(Bukhari)

رَبِّ قِنِي عَذَابَكَ يَوْمَ تَبْعَثُ عِبَادَكَ

Rabbi qinee ‘adhaabaka yawma tab’athu ‘ibaadak

My Lord, protect me from Your punishment on the Day You resurrect Your servants.

(Tirmidhi)

بِاسْمِكَ رَبِّي وَضَعْتُ جَنْبِي، وَبِكَ أَرْفَعُهُ

Bismika Rabbi wada’tu janbee, wabika arfa’uh

In Your name, my Lord, I lay down my side and in Your name I rise it.

(Bukhari)

اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَسْأَلُكَ خَيْرَ هَذِهِ اللَّيْلَةِ

Allahumma inni as’aluka khayra hadhihi allayla

O Allah, I ask You for the good of this night.

(Tirmidhi)

اللَّهُمَّ أَنْتَ خَلَقْتَ نَفْسِي، وَأَنْتَ تَوَفَّاهَا

Allahumma anta khalaqta nafsee wa anta tawaffaha

O Allah, You created my soul and You will take it back.

(Muslim)
